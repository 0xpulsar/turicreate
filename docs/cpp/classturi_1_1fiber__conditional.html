Detailed Description

Wrapper around pthread's condition variable, that can work with both fibers and threads simultaneously, but at a cost of much greater memory requirements.

Limitations.

  • Does not support timed wait
  • threads and fibers are not queued perfectly. fibers are preferentially signaled.
